Behavior 1
--User enters a word
--Program will  determine 1 count

Behavior 2
--User enters a sentence or more than on word.
--Program will count how many words total and determine if there are any repetitive words

Behavior 3
--User enters nothing
--Program will print out a message "Please enter a word or sentence(s)"

Behavior 4
--User enters special characters only
--Program will ignore and  will print out a message "Please enter a word or sentence(s)"

Behavior 5
--User enters a word or sentence and special characters
--Program will count how many words total and ignore the special characters
